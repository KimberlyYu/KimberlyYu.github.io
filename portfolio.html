Arcadia High Mobile App

After almost a year of work, AHS Mobile App was successfully launched on August 13, 2020. It provides Arcadia students, parents, and staff access to important school information and resources. Currently, we have 775 daily users and put out almost 30 articles every week combined with each article's own push notifications. The front-end of the iOS app was built using Swift and Obj-C while the Android app was made with Java. Graphic Designers created UI/UX designs, which were translated to code by the programmers.

Arcadia High Mobile Content Editing Website

In order to input articles, remove articles, and send notifications to the user's phone, we needed a backend. Thus, my sister and I created the Content Editing Website using html, css, and javascript. We used Firebase's authentication, hosting, and notification services to sign users in and out, deploy and host our website, and send push notifications to the many people in Arcadia who use the app.

StressLief - Mental Health Awareness App

StressLief—a term I coined by joining the words Stress and Relief together. This project was initiated by the AHS App Probation Team as a project highlighting our collaboration and efficiency. Mental health is a large problem at our school where numerous students only have less than 6 hours of sleep per day due to high workloads and stress. With this app, we hope to help provide articles on ways to combat procrastination, depression, stress, and many other issues.
